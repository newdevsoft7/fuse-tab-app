Check In / Out Module
Enable the check in / out module to allow your staff to check in and out of shifts to indicate their arrival and departure.
Check In Open
{{option}}
Enter the amount in minutes before a shift start time after which a staff can check in.
Check In Photo
Enable to require staff to upload a photo upon check in.
Check Out Photo
Enable to require staff to upload a photo upon check out.
Late Check In No Show
{{option}}
Enter the amount in minutes after a shift start time after which a staff who has not checked in will be assigned the 'No Show' status.
